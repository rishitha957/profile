Rishitha Kalicheti

I am a master's in Computer Science student at The Johns Hopkins University, Whiting School of Engineering, where I am currently enrolled in courses such as NLP: Self-Supervised Methods, Machine Translation and Machine Learning . I am currently doing my internship at Leidos as a Software Developer, working with OCSS (Office of Child Support Services), developing their CI/CD pipeling, and intergrating features such as code autocomplete etc, to improve developer productivity.

From 2021 to 2023, I worked as a Software Developer at UBS. I was part of CEDA (Core Engineering Data and Analytics) team, where I developed and optimized our Data Reporting and Governance Platform. Additionally, exposure to complex financial systems helped me design robust systems that handle large-scale data. I was also exposed to Azure cloud to design and deploy our solutions. I am certified as an Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900) professional.

I have a Bachelor's (BTech) degree in Computer Science from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Tirupati, where I was an undergrad researcher at RISHA (Research in Intelligent Systems and Human Analytics) Lab headed by Dr. Sridhar Chimalakonda where I designed tools that help developers in improving software quality by analysing a wide range of software artifacts. I also worked as an ML Researcher as part of Dr. Kalidas's Lab.
